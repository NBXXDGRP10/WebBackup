Who We Are

We are Team Grape come from The University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC), aiming at doing the full year Group Project (GRP). "Be the best team" is our motto. The reason why we called 'Grape' is because grapes live together and every one has its own part.

Team Work

The highs of a project are more motivating when working as a team. Celebrating an achievement with teammates is a great way to boost morale. If you work alone, who are you going to high-five when you get something working?
